Welcome to Brainiacs Learning

At Brainiacs, education is a private matter between the learner and the world of knowledge.

With a commitment to bring out the best in every child, we treat every learner as a unique individual with a unique style and pace of learning. We believe learning should not be limited to teacher-student contact hours, neither should parents replicate a formal classroom session in a home setting. Rather, after school learning should be geared towards personalised learning that is fun, accelerated and improves digital literacy. Hence, our use of Educational technologies to promote learning amongst pupils and students from KG through to Senior High School.
